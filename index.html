Hello,

I m a PhD student at UCLouvain (Belgium).

I started this journey in 2018 and the adventure is ongoing. The all project is about phenotyping the hydraulic on the root system. In that regards, we work with models and we go from cells-scale to plant-scale. The methodology that we are building will help estimate the transpiration and even the global effect of local changes.
